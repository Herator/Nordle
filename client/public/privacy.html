Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Nordle handles information when you use the Discord Activity.

1. Information We Collect

When you authorize Nordle through Discord, we receive the following from Discord's OAuth2 API:

  • Your Discord user ID
  • Your Discord username
  • Your Discord avatar hash

We also collect your in-game activity: the colored results of each word guess (green/yellow/gray). The actual letters you type are never stored or shared.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your name and avatar in the live player sidebar within the activity
  • To post your guess progress (colored tiles only) to the Discord channel if you use the /nordle command
  • To restore your daily game progress if you reopen the activity during the same day

3. Data Storage

All data is stored in memory only on the server hosting the activity. It is never written to a database or disk. All data is automatically cleared when the server restarts or at the start of each new day.

We do not use cookies, local storage, or any client-side persistence.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties. Your Discord username, avatar, and guess results are only visible to other players participating in the same Nordle activity session within your Discord server.

When you use /nordle, your guess progress (colored tiles, no letters) is posted to the Discord channel via Discord's bot API. This is controlled by you and can be avoided by simply not using the command.

5. Third-Party Services

Nordle uses Discord's API and OAuth2. Please review Discord's own Privacy Policy for how Discord handles your data.

6. Children's Privacy

Nordle does not knowingly collect data from anyone under the age of 13, in line with Discord's own minimum age requirements.

7. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated at any time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

8. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via the Discord server where you use Nordle.
